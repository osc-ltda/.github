## 🛠 Our Process: From Idea to Solution

1. **Understand your idea:** We discuss the problem and define what success looks like.
2. **Exploration & Scope:** Prioritize the essential and detect risks early.
3. **Agreement:** Functional and technical requirements in a live, understandable document.
4. **Design & Architecture:** We choose a minimal architecture that scales.
5. **Iterative Construction:** Short sprints, real demos, and constant feedback.
6. **Hardening & Testing:** Functional and automated tests to ensure stability.
7. **Production Deployment:** We go live in the cloud—reliable, fast, and stable.
8. **Operation or Handoff:** We can continue monitoring or transfer the code with a clear guide.
